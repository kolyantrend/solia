--- Full Description ---

Solia transforms your ideas into revenue. Powered by cutting-edge AI image generation and built on the Solana blockchain, Solia lets anyone create professional-quality visuals from simple text prompts — and instantly monetize them.

Key Features:

• AI Image Generation — Turn text prompts into stunning images across 19+ categories including Anime, Cyberpunk, Fantasy, 3D, Photography, and more. Choose from multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1, and others).

• Earn From Your Art — Every image you publish to the global feed can be purchased by other users. Creators receive 80% of each sale directly to their Solana wallet, instantly and trustlessly.

• Non-Custodial & Secure — Connect any Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, and more). Solia never has access to your private keys. All transactions happen on-chain with full transparency.

• Social Features — Like, comment, follow your favorite creators, and climb the leaderboard. Build your audience and grow your creative reputation.

• Image Protection — All published images are watermarked for protection. Only buyers and creators can access original unwatermarked files and prompts.

• Referral Program — Invite friends and earn 10% of every purchase they make on the platform. Share your unique referral link and watch your earnings grow.

• Built on Solana — Lightning-fast transactions with minimal fees. Payments are processed in SKR tokens on the Solana blockchain.

Solia is the creative economy for the AI generation. Whether you're an artist, a meme creator, or just someone with great ideas — your imagination is now your income.
